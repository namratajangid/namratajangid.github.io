Hema Chowdhary




Hema runs an Event Management Company and lives with her family and three dogs.

Hema regularly feeds and cares for more than thirty five dogs in her locality.

She cannot stand animal cruelty in any form and is up in arms when the need arises to protect these defenceless creatures.



Pallavi Chopra




Pallavi is an avid animal lover. She has a background in teaching.

Pallavi lives with her husband and a dog.

She feeds and cares for almost 20 dogs in her locality and is extremely emotionally attached to every puppy or kitten that she sets her eyes on.




Aparna Mishra




Aparna was born and brought up in an animal loving family in Kanpur. Living in a Cantt area ended up feeding dogs, monkeys, goats, cows, peacocks, birds and squirrels.

In Mumbai after marriage made friends with all the strays nearby and after meeting Shirley and joining SOS started medicating them as well. “I am a model co-ordinator and aim to open a state of the art animal hospital some day and increase my association with animal lovers.” – Says Aparna
