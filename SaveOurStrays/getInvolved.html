Be a Volunteer for SOS


SOS aims to create a society where both animals and humans live in harmony. Come be part of the SOS team by volunteering in the various animal welfare programs undertaken by us and express your love for animals by helping them. Be the change you wish to see, do your bit to create a society where animals live in peace.


You can Volunteer in various ways:


  • Be a part of the First Aid team where you will be briefed about common injuries and ailments in animals and how they can be treated.

  • Facilitate adoptions of dogs and cats by publicizing with the Press, Vets,other animal based NGO's and Veterinary Chemists; coordinate with the potential adopters and do regular follow ups after the adoption has taken place.

  • Assist in identifying dogs and cats that have to be sterilized area-wise and enlist the support of residents and shopkeepers in those areas for after care of the animals.

  • Collect funds, medicines and food that can be used to support and sustain our animal welfare programme.



Sponsor an Animal


Over the years, the Save Our Strays ambulance has rescued many blind, handicapped and old cats and dogs from the streets of Mumbai. Most of them could not be released back on the streets for fear of being run over or being killed. This led to the "Sponsor An Animal" Project, which is our biggest commitment at SOS, and the one closest to our heart. We love our lesser privileged ones and strive to keep them safe and happy for the rest of their lives.
