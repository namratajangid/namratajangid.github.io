Shirley Menon




Shirley Menon is the founder of SOS. Shirley comes from a animal loving middle class family. She has a background in the service industry. However, she quit her full time job to dedicate more time to SOS.

Shirley's aim in life is to see animals and humans living in harmony the world over. She firmly believes that most people may not be able to change the world, but each one of us can make a difference in our lifetime.



Namrata Kamath





Namrata Kamath, a housewife and an animal lover who lives with her family, one cat and two dogs.


Namrata has a magical touch with animals. She strangely talks to them in Konkani which is her mother tongue and they all seem to understand it.

Inara Shahnawaz

Amit Gajaria




Amit is a chemical engineer by profession and running his family business. He has been an ardent animal lover since childhood.

Rinky Karmarkar

Rinky is amazingly emotional about what she does and passionately goes about doing her work.
